Life can be overwhelming.

This book
can help.

This isn't your typical calendar. It's made for kids who split their time between two homes — a place to color-code your weeks, track special days, and make sense of all the moving parts.

  1. 1 Color code your days. Who's picking up? When's the swap? Mark Home 1 and Home 2 so the whole week makes sense at a glance.
  2. 2 Add fun things: Yes Days, sleepovers. Write them in or use the stickers!
  3. 3 Don't forget the important stuff: haircuts, doctor visits, you name it.
  4. 4 Use the 'tools' pages to help with big feelings.
  5. 5 Have fun!!! There are lots of spaces to just get creative.
