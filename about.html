About.

A book for homies with 2 homes.

Part calendar, part workbook, part journal, part coloring book — and a heavy dose of you've-got-this. Made for kids who know exactly what it means to have two bedrooms, two sets of rules, and two homes that both belong to them.

The Story

Made because nothing else existed.

Kirsten's daughter Sailor was getting overwhelmed. She still does gymnastics every week — but who's picking her up? When is she swapping homes? She was too young for a phone, and traditional calendars weren't made for her life.

There are wall calendars. There are school planners. But nothing that travels with a kid from home to home, that's actually theirs — designed for the reality of two schedules, two bedrooms, two families who love them.

So Kirsten and Chris made it. A yearlong calendar for kids ages 6–14 that goes everywhere they go. Part schedule tracker, part feelings helper, part coloring book — and 100% theirs.

"Both homes are yours. Full stop."
Co-Founder

Kirsten Ludwig

Mom, Divorcee & Thought Leader — Los Angeles

Kirsten knows this world from the inside. As a mom and a divorcee raising her kid across two homes in LA, she felt the gap between what existed and what kids actually needed.

2 Homies is her answer to the tool she wished she could have handed her kid years ago.

Co-Founder

Chris Danton

Mom, Ally & Creative Director — Europe

Chris is a mom, creative director, and strategist who brought the vision to life with brains and heart. Living in Europe, she's a long-distance collaborator with a very close perspective on what this project means.

She's here as an ally — and because great tools for kids deserve great design.

The Artist

Maxine McCrann

Maxine is the illustrator who made 2 Homies feel the way it does. Every feeling wheel, every light bulb, every little house — that's her. Based in Toronto, she's a resident color enthusiast whose work is warm, playful, and impossible to ignore.

She's the reason kids open the book and don't want to put it down.
